About Us

Karthick Skin And Hair Clinic

Karthick Skin and Hair Clinic, located on Tambaram-Velachery Main Road in Gowrivakkam, Chennai, is a trusted dermatology practice led by the experienced and compassionate Dr. Balasubramaniam. With a stellar 4.7-star rating from over 146 patients, the clinic is renowned for its thorough consultations, personalized treatment plans, and a philosophy that prioritizes natural healing over unnecessary medications. From common skin rashes to chronic hair conditions and pediatric skin concerns, the clinic serves patients of all ages with equal care and dedication.

What truly sets Karthick Skin and Hair Clinic apart is Dr. Balasubramaniam's unwavering commitment to every patient — often staying well beyond closing hours to ensure no one goes unattended. The clinic operates on a token-based system to maintain order and fairness, offering both morning and evening consultation slots on most weekdays. Whether you are visiting for the first time or managing a long-term condition, you can expect a warm, professional environment where your health is always the top priority.
